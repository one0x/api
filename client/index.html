About Us

Peerbuds is a self-governed decentralized platform that helps you gain control and ownership of your knowledge story.

Whether it’s a learning experience with a teacher, an online session with a peer, a community discussion or any other source; on peerbuds, the right amount of information becomes part of a growing public ledger of the world’s learning and impact.

You control who gets to see your story or what parts of it.

Every activity is instantly validated against rules set in stone by a teacher or the community and is accessible all lifelong.

All knowledge is reflected on your profile as Gyan - the new standard unit of knowledge that equals about an hour of learning and sums your knowledge score on any topic.

Gyan’s mission is to empower every student, learner, institution and employer with a trusted, traceable and easily shareable knowledge score.
